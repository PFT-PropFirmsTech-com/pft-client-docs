<objective>
CRM-01: Capture an arbitrary partner `clickid` at a tracking-link entry point and bridge the anonymous click to the eventual signup. A new `GET /track` route sets a first-party `_partner_clickid` cookie (30-day, mirroring the existing ad-platform cookie pattern) and 302-redirects to the site. The pft-dashboard signup flow reads that cookie on the register call and forwards it unchanged as `partnerClickId` in the request body â€” where the backend registration persistence (plan 10-02) picks it up.

Purpose: The user is anonymous at click time; identity only exists after OTP verification. A first-party cookie set on landing + forwarded as a signup body field is the reliable anonymous-click â†’ account bridge. This plan builds ONLY the capture + forward plumbing (schema/persist are 10-02/10-03).

Output: A working `/track` redirect+cookie route on the backend, and a signup payload that carries `partnerClickId` when the cookie is present.
</objective>
